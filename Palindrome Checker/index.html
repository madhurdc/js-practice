Palindrome Checker

All non-alphanumeric characters are removed (like spaces, %, !, @, #, etc.)

Example strings to try:
Palindromes:

  • 0_0 (: /-\ :) 0-0
  • race !)$! (@* $)!@($*)!) car
  • M @ aDA ! M
  • A man, a plan, a canal. Panama
Not Palindromes:
  • five|\_/|four
  • race 123 @ car
  • not a Palindrome
